<h2 id="write-when">When to write</h2>
<ul>
<li>In the writing window — the deliberate sessions set aside for it.</li>
<li>When <strong>recognizing connections</strong> — while writing other notes, or when a real-life situation triggers an idea that already lives in the vault. This one rests on habits built <em>outside</em> the writing sessions: an intimate mental map of your own vault, and the reflex of spotting links between a fleeting thought and the ideas already living there.</li>
</ul>
