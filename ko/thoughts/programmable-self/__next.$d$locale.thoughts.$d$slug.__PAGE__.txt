5:T42a,<h2>The Mindset</h2>
<p>Just do it, feel it, get relieved of trusting your system.
Remember that tangible results take time and patience.</p>
<h2>The Components</h2>
<p>There are a couple of programmatic MindOS components:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Calendar</strong> is the <strong>process scheduler</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>To-do lists</strong> are the <strong>wait queues</strong>.</li>
<li>All the mental models: <strong>Identities</strong>, <strong>Principles</strong>, <strong>Mental Models</strong>, <strong>Operation Modes/Contexts</strong> are just implementation frameworks.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Type of Processes</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Programs</strong>: They should be preemptive with timers.</li>
<li><strong>Reflection</strong>: A meta program at the start and end of a cycle. This is the source of MindOS plasticity.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Qualities</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Reliability</strong>: how instructions are adhered to. In other words, discipline.</li>
</ul>
