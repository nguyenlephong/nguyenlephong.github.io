<ul>

<p>The fulfillment of the "<strong>this is it</strong>" moment. When I enjoy life as it is. When the present is all I have, and it is enough.</p>

<li>You can feel the imbalance happening. You by instinct, feel the urge to restore the balance, you like things balanced.</li>
